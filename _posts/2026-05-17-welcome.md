This is the first post in the site's publication archive. The blog is designed to work like a personal Substack-style space: essays can be drafted in the CMS, published as Markdown, tagged by topic, and displayed in a clean reading layout.

Future posts can include images, links, footnotes, and longer-form arguments while remaining portable because the content lives in plain text files inside the GitHub repository.
